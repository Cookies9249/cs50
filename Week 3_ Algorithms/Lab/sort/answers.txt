sort1 uses: Bubble Sort

How do you know?:
50k reversed: 5.803s, 50k sorted: 0.983s
In bubble, reversed takes long (n^2) while sorted takes a very short time (n)

sort2 uses: Merge Sort

How do you know?:
50k reversed: 1.611s, 50k sorted: 1.267s
In merge, both reversed and sorted take a short time (n log n)

sort3 uses: Selection Sort

How do you know?:
50k reversed: 4.081, 50k sorted: 3.689s
In selection, both reversed and sorted take long (n^2)
